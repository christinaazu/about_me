meet the brands

The Rail + Co.

First and foremost, we wanted to create show-inspired apparel that suits the needs and tastes of the performance horse culture. Our collection embodies the off-duty equestrian and our designs are tailor made to go seamlessly from stable to city. Influenced by the timeless sophistication of english riding and vintage feel of western disciplines, the Rail + Co. tells a story of who you are and where you're from. We're passionate about showing and want the world to know it.

On The Rail Photo + Design

A photograph is the perfect tool to preserve your memories and capture the joys in your life. For many of us, our joy comes in the form of horses, family, and friends. I want to provide each of my clients with dazzling photographs they can cherish, share, and enjoy.

Custom advertising and design holds value both in and out of the show pen. Simply put, I love design! More than that, I love creating charming artwork for my clients and their beloved teammates. Ads are a great tool for expressing your achievements, solidifying your future goals, and presenting your best self to the competitive market.
